Three Tips to Supercharge Your Research

We've seen researchers get the most out of Open Paper when they use these three powerful strategies. Try them on your next paper reading!

📋

Start with Paper Briefs

Use briefs as a guiding point for starting your reading. They provide an efficient overview to help you get context before diving into detailed sections.

Pro tip: Review the brief first, then jot down 2-3 questions you want to answer from the paper.
🎧

Leverage Audio Overviews

Educational research shows that engaging multiple senses enhances memory retention. Our audio overviews let you absorb research while commuting, exercising, or doing chores.

Learn why audio boosts research efficiency →

💬

Ask Questions Liberally

When you get stuck, use chat as your research copilot. Ask it to find specific citations, explain complex concepts, or connect the ideas to your priors.

Pro tip: Try questions like "What evidence supports the main claim?" or "What are some shortcomings of the methodology?"
